0x offers professional-grade APIs that aggregate and enable swaps at the best price on millions of tokens across 150+ DEXes on your favorite EVM-compatible chains. 0x is trusted by Coinbase, Matcha, Phantom, DefiLlama, Robinhood Wallet, and [many more](https://0x.org/case-studies).

See our list of [supported chains](/docs/introduction/supported-chains).

## New to 0x?

- We recommend starting off with an [Introduction to 0x](/docs/core-concepts/introduction-to-0x).
- Get started for free, visit the [0x Dashboard](https://dashboard.0x.org/) to create an account and get your live API keys.
